  2. Allow Location Access - Grant permission for location services

  5. Stay Informed - Receive notifications about outages in your area

┌─ ABOUT ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

Apagón is a community-driven mobile application that allows residents of Puerto Rico to report their electric power status and view real-time outage reports from across the island.

Initially developed after Hurricane María as a response to poor government data infrastructure for tracking power outages, Apagón empowers citizens to document and visualize electrical service interruptions quickly and effortlessly.

Available in English and Spanish for both iOS and Android devices.
